Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your pizzas authentic Detroit-style?

I grew up in metro Detroit making this pizza long before anyone called it “Detroit-style.” At Woodward, we stay true to that tradition: every pizza is baked in seasoned steel pans that give it a crisp bottom and light, airy interior. We spread the cheese all the way to the edges for the caramelized frico crust, then finish with warm sauce on top after the bake. It’s the same style I remember from back home, now shared here in Edmond and the OKC metro.

Do you have vegan options?

We never want anyone to feel left out because of their diet. That’s why we offer vegan cheese, plant-based meats, and fresh, in-house cut veggies so you can build a pizza that fits your lifestyle. We even create the occasional Vegan Pizza of the Month to keep things exciting and make sure all of our guests feel taken care of.

Do you have dairy-free options?

Yes — we know how important dairy-free choices are, and for us it’s personal. When our daughter was a newborn, she had a minor dairy allergy, so we understand the need firsthand. At Woodward Pizza we offer dairy-free cheese and plenty of fresh toppings, and many of our pizzas can be customized into a dairy-free pizza without losing any of the flavor. Our goal is to make sure everyone — whether you’re local to Edmond or visiting the OKC metro — can enjoy a great slice together.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes — and this one is close to our hearts. I spent over 9 months researching and developing our gluten-free dough, determined to make it almost indistinguishable from our standard Detroit-style pizza. The result is light, fluffy, crisp, and deep — just like the square pizzas I grew up on. Our original business partner has celiac disease, and when he tried it for the first time he told me it was the best gluten-free pizza he’d ever had. To go with it, we also keep gluten-free cider on tap and carry gluten-free beer. In our kitchen we take extra precautions: a separate mixer for gluten-free dough, different pans, and we prepare the dough later in the day so regular flour dust has time to settle. While we can’t guarantee a completely flour-free environment, we do everything possible to minimize cross-contact and create a safe, delicious option for guests with gluten sensitivity.

Do you have a full bar?

Yes — Woodward Pizza has a full bar that’s all about bringing people together. We serve craft cocktails, local Oklahoma beers, and rotating seasonal drinks that pair perfectly with our Detroit-style pizzas. Whether you’re relaxing on the patio with friends, grabbing a local pint before heading downtown, or enjoying date night in Edmond, our bar is designed to be a welcoming spot for the whole community.

Do you serve dessert?

Yes — we’ve got plenty of ways to end your meal on a sweet note. Our cinnamon sticks come with house-made orange icing and can even be ordered gluten-free. We rotate seasonal flavors of freshly churned gelato, including dairy-free options, and we hand-fill our classic cannolis. And for the grown-ups, we say a well-crafted cocktail can count as dessert too — our bar team always has something seasonal to sip alongside your square.

Do you take reservations?

Our pizzeria is a small, community space, so we stay first-come, first-served to keep it fair and relaxed for everyone. For larger groups, there’s an event space right next to our back door called The Stables at Icehouse. We can connect you with their team and provide catering so your whole group can enjoy our Detroit-style pizza together.

Is Woodward Pizza kid-friendly?

Yes — we’re family-owned, and our own kids are often around helping out when they’re not in school. We welcome families to come enjoy pizza together, as long as the little ones keep from tossing rocks at each other and don’t try climbing on the roof.

Do you have high chairs or booster seats?

Yes — we keep high chairs on hand so even our youngest guests can enjoy pizza night with the family. Just let our team know when you arrive, and we’ll get you set up.

Do you have a kids’ menu?

We’re a pizzeria — every pizza can be a kids’ meal! A 4-square cheese pizza is the perfect size for dinner tonight and leftovers for tomorrow. Plus, kids love finishing with gelato or a cinnamon stick for dessert.

Do you do anything fun for kids?

Yes! We want Woodward Pizza to be more than just dinner — it’s a fun family outing. Kids can grab a coloring sheet or a temporary tattoo while they wait, cool off with gelato after their meal, and even climb on the giant green grasshopper play structure right outside. We love being a place where kids make memories, not just eat pizza.

Can kids hang out on the patio?

Yes — kids are welcome on the patio as long as they’re accompanied by an adult. The courtyard is a great spot for families to relax: adults can sip a cold beer or cocktail while the kids play. We just ask that there are no jailbreaks or car hijackings — this isn’t 8 Mile in Detroit.

What desserts do kids love the most?

Most kids go straight for the gelato counter — especially when we roll out fun seasonal flavors, including dairy-free options. Our warm cinnamon sticks with house-made orange icing are another favorite, and here’s a pro-tip: add a side of cannoli filling for dipping. Kids love it, and parents usually end up stealing a bite too.

Are strollers welcome?

Yes — strollers are welcome at Woodward Pizza. We know dining out with little ones can be an adventure, so we keep things family-friendly. Our patio and courtyard make it easy to bring a stroller along, and our team will help you find a comfortable spot inside or outside. Just don’t try to parallel park it at the bar — the wheels don’t fit between the barstools.

What’s the best pizza for kids?

Most kids go straight for our classic cheese or pepperoni 4-square pizzas — the perfect size for dinner tonight and leftovers tomorrow. For adventurous eaters, the Spicy Hawaiian without the jalapeños is a big hit. Fair warning though: kids tend to demolish a cheese square faster than parents can sneak a bite.

Do you have outdoor seating for families?

Yes — our patio is perfect for families. Adults can relax with a cold drink while kids burn off energy in the courtyard or on the giant green grasshopper climbing structure. Kids get to climb, parents get to sip — everyone wins.

Do you have anything for picky eaters?

Yes — we’ve got plenty of kid-friendly choices. Our 4-square cheese or pepperoni pizzas are always a hit, but if your kiddo isn’t feeling pizza we’ve also got breadsticks and meatballs. And of course, a scoop of gelato usually solves everything.

Do you have a lunch special?

Yes — we offer a weekday lunch special, Monday through Friday from 11:00am to 2:00pm. It’s designed to be quick and satisfying, with options like a classic cheese or pepperoni square, our rotating slice of the day, or pairings such as slice and half salad or even a salad and meatball combo. It’s the perfect way to enjoy Detroit-style pizza (or a lighter option) on your lunch break in Edmond or the OKC metro.

Do you sell pizza by the slice?

Yes — we serve up Detroit-style pizza by the slice during our weekday lunch hours, Monday through Friday from 11:00am to 2:00pm. The slice of the day changes, so it’s your chance to cheat on your go-to pizza and try something new without committing to a whole pan.

Do you offer online ordering?

Yes — you can order Woodward Pizza online for pickup or delivery right from our website. In fact, our online menu has even more options than our in-store menu — kind of like a cheat code or secret menu for pizza fans. Whether it’s lunch, family dinner, or game night, online ordering makes it quick and easy to get your Detroit-style favorites.

Where can I park when visiting Woodward Pizza?

Parking is easy when you visit us at the Icehouse Project. There’s a shared parking lot right by the building, but even if the lot is full, we still have plenty of seating — especially on the patio. You can also park in the adjoining street spots or walk over from the Farmers Market parking just down the way.

Can I order Woodward Pizza while I’m at American Solera?

Yes! If you’re hanging out at American Solera next door, you can order from our menu right at your table using the QR codes. Place your order, and we’ll bring your Detroit-style pizza over to you so you can enjoy a craft beer with a hot pizza without ever leaving your seat.

Can I order from the patio?

Yes! Whether you’re feeling impatient or just enjoying your conversation with that blind date, you can order straight from your patio table. Just scan the QR code on the table, select your table number, and place your order. We’ll bring your food right out to you so you never have to leave your seat.

Do you offer catering or large orders?

Yes — we’ve got you covered whether it’s a family gathering, an office lunch, or feeding the whole football team. From Detroit-style square pizzas to salads and sides, we can put together a spread that keeps everyone happy. Just give us a call ahead of time so we can plan and have it ready for your crew.

Do you have a happy hour?

Yes — we actually have two happy hours every Monday through Friday: from 2:00–6:00pm and again from 8:30–10:00pm. It’s the perfect time to stop by for a cocktail or a beer to pair with your Detroit-style pizza.
